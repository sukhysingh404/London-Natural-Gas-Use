This website is to showcase two maps I created with public datasets. London gas sales 2001 exists as a predecestor to the more informative London gas sales by year. This webpage exists entirely to allow me to host these maps on GitHub.

The first map shows London's domestic gas sales per consumer by borough in the year 2001. The second map takes this a step further, showing how domestic gas sales have changed from 2001 to 2021.
